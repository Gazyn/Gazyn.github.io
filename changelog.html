Update 8
Tons of balance changes!
Reworked the visuals
Added healer crit chance
Block chance scales at 1% per upgrade instead of 3%
Crit chance scales at 1% per upgrade instead of 3%

Healing cost reduction upgrade is now -5.5% from -5.2%
Max mana upgrade is now 4.895% from 5%
This should keep the mana cost in line with max mana at all levels.

Monster gold scales at 35% per level instead of 34%
Healer cast speed general upgrade is less effective at the start, more effective later.
You no longer start with 1 attack speed upgrade purchased at the start
Damage reduction upgrade has been renamed to armor. Functionality is the same.
Upgrades cost scales 17.5% per level from 18%

Archer and enemy attack speed is now seperate!
Enemy attack speed scales with the general upgrade and caps at 2/s
